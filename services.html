What I Offer

Over 25 years of clinical practice, research, and policy work inform a comprehensive suite of psychological services — delivered with rigour, cultural sensitivity, and deep expertise in the Maldivian context.

01
Clinical Consultation
Specialist mental health consultation for children, adolescents, and adults. Comprehensive assessment and evidence-based psychological treatment for anxiety, depression, trauma, grief, interpersonal difficulties, and complex presentations.
AdultsAdolescentsChildren
02
Psychological Assessment & Profiling
Comprehensive cognitive, neuropsychological, diagnostic, and personality assessments. Over 17,500 psychological profiles authored across 25+ years. Specialised evaluations for neurodevelopmental conditions including ADHD, autism spectrum disorder, and learning disabilities.
CognitiveNeuropsychologicalPersonalityDiagnostic
03
Expert Witness & Court Psychology
The first psychologist in the Maldives to provide expert evidence in court. Expert witness services for civil and criminal proceedings, fitness-to-stand-trial evaluations, risk assessments, and victim impact assessments.
Expert WitnessRisk AssessmentCourt Reports
04
Victim Profiling
Psychological profiling of victims of crime, including survivors of domestic violence, sexual abuse, and gang-related violence. Over 300 victim-of-crime profiles completed for judicial and rehabilitation purposes.
Crime VictimsDomestic ViolenceSexual Abuse
05
Offender Rehabilitation
Design and delivery of rehabilitation and reintegration programmes for offenders, including gang members and incarcerated youth. Counselling, group therapy, family interventions, and structured pathways to education, skills, and employment.
ReintegrationGang ExitCorrections
06
CBT · DBT · EMDR
Evidence-based psychotherapies including Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, Dialectical Behaviour Therapy, and Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing. Delivered within a culturally sensitive framework adapted for Maldivian and South Asian contexts.
TraumaAnxietyDepression
07
Training & Workshops
Professional workshops on mental health awareness, parenting skills, life skills and communication, bullying prevention, special needs support, drug abuse prevention, and violence prevention. Tailored for students, youth, parents, teachers, religious scholars, community leaders, policy makers, law enforcement, and correctional services.
ParentingLife SkillsPreventionSpecial Needs

Policy & Strategic Planning
National mental health strategic planning, service development, and institutional capacity building. Consultation to government ministries, international organisations, and NGOs. Author of the National Mental Health Strategic Action Plan 2016–2021 for the Maldives.

Television, radio, and digital media programming on mental health and social issues. Producer and presenter of Dr. Naazge Nazarun and other landmark broadcast series. Weekly TikTok content on psychological issues affecting children, parents, and youth.

Keynote presentations, academic lectures, professional workshops, and research collaboration. Experienced trainer for mental health professionals, educators, and justice sector personnel. Available for international and regional speaking engagements.
